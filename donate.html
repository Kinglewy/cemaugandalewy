Support the Grassroots Climate Action Fund (GCAF)

Empower Communities. Protect the Environment. Drive Change. Communities on the frontline of climate change are already leading solutions—protecting forests, monitoring environmental violations, and advocating for accountability. Yet, they often lack the resources to sustain and scale their impact. CEMA’s Grassroots Climate Action Fund (GCAF) supports these local champions with small, flexible grants to advance climate justice, environmental accountability, and community resilience.
Why Your Support Matters
Your contribution helps
• Fund community-led climate solutions
• Strengthen environmental accountability
• Support women and youth on the frontlines
• Amplify marginalized voices in climate governance
• Protect ecosystems and livelihoods
What Your Donation Can Do
$50 – Supports community awareness and mobilization
$150 – Funds local environmental monitoring activities
$500 – Supports a grassroots advocacy initiative
$1,000+ – Contributes to a full small grant for a community project
Donate Now – Be the Spark of Hope Every contribution powers progress. Your gift will directly help refugees gain access to clean, renewable energy—and a brighter future.
Donor Recognition: Unless they prefer to remain anonymous, donors will be acknowledged on CEMA’s website and in Grassroots Climate Action Fund (GCAF) project communication and visibility materials.
