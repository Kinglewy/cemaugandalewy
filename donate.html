Bring Clean Energy to Refugee Families in Uganda

Light Up Lives with Clean, Sustainable Energy In Uganda’s refugee settlements, thousands of families still live without safe, reliable electricity. They rely on dangerous kerosene lamps or walk long distances to charge phones—putting children and women at risk and limiting opportunities for study, safety, and economic activity. You can change that.
At Centre for Environment and Migration Assistance, we’re on a mission to provide solar lanterns, clean cookstoves, and community solar hubs to refugee families. With your support, we can power lives with dignity, safety, and sustainability.
What Your Donation Can Do: • $20 provides a solar lantern for a family • $50 provides a clean cookstove to reduce smoke inhalation • $100 powers a community charging station • $500 installs a solar mini-grid for a learning center
Why It Matters: • Reduces health risks from indoor air pollution • Enhances safety at night, especially for women and children • Supports education—children can study after dark • Enables small business and communication opportunities • Real Impact: • "Before we got the solar lamp, my children couldn’t study at night. Now they dream of becoming doctors and teachers." – Rose, Refugee Mother, Kyaka II Settlement.
Donate Now – Be the Spark of Hope Every contribution powers progress. Your gift will directly help refugees gain access to clean, renewable energy—and a brighter future.
Other Ways to Help: • Sponsor a Solar Kit for a classroom or health post • Start a fundraiser with your company or school • Share this page and spread the light

Help Refugee Children in Uganda Rebuild Their Futures


Over 1.7 million refugees have fled to Uganda seeking safety from conflict and crisis. Among them are thousands of children—many of whom arrived alone, traumatized, and without access to education, healthcare, or emotional support Your Support Can Change a Life At Centre for Environment and Migration Assistance, we are working to provide safe shelters, education, nutritious meals, trauma counseling, and a future full of possibilities for these vulnerable children. But we can't do it without you.
How Your Donation Helps: • $25 provides school supplies for one child for a term • $50 covers a month's worth of nutritious meals • $100 supports trauma counseling for a child in need • $250 helps build a child-friendly learning center in a settlement • Real Stories. Real Impact. • “I didn’t think I would ever go back to school. Now I can read and write, and I want to become a teacher.” – Amina, age 11, Kyangwali Refugee Settlement Donate Now – Be Their Light of Hope. • All donations go directly toward programs helping refugee children in Kyaka II, Kyangwali and Rwamwnja Settlements. Your gift today can create a better tomorrow.
Other Ways to Help • Start a fundraiser with your friends and family • Sponsor a child’s education for a year • Share this page to spread the word
