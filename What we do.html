What We do

CEMA works at the intersection of humaritarian response and long-term development to ensure that displaced populations, especially refugees, women and youth are only protected but empowered as agents of change in their communities

Resilient Inclusive and Sustainable Energy (RISE)
In Uganda

CEMA actively responds to this challenge by implementing sustainable environmental interventions in refugee-hosting areas. These include promoting the use of alternative energy sources such as fuel-efficient cookstoves and solar technologies, supporting reforestation and agroforestry programs, and training both refugees and host communities in sustainable land management practices
